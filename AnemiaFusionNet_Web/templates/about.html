About HemoVision AI

AI-Powered Multimodal Anemia Detection System using Eye Images, Clinical Parameters and Geographic Risk Analysis.

About the Project

HemoVision AI is an intelligent healthcare platform designed to assist in the early detection of anemia using Artificial Intelligence. The system combines retinal/conjunctival eye image analysis with clinical blood parameters and regional geographic risk factors to improve prediction accuracy. The objective is to provide fast, affordable and accessible anemia screening while supporting healthcare professionals with AI-driven insights.

🎯 Our Mission

To enable affordable, rapid and AI-assisted anemia screening, especially in underserved and rural communities.

👁 Our Vision

To become an intelligent healthcare platform capable of assisting medical professionals through multimodal AI diagnosis and smart clinical decision support.
